About Me

I'm a South London-based filmmaker with a passion for telling relevant and politically charged stories. I'm particularly interested in documentary filmmaking as a tool for progressive change. I believe in the importance of community — in its resilience, diversity, and the challenges it faces — and I aim to capture these realities with honesty and empathy.

I'm a professional, organised communicator with solid experience on semi-professional sets. I have hands-on knowledge across multiple areas of production and never shy away from getting stuck in wherever I'm needed.
